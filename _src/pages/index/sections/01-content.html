The night, plainly

There is nothing to do
and nothing to say

  1. 01

    Descent

    You lie down on a mat among twenty or so people who came for the same reason you did. For the better part of an hour there is only sound, most of it pitched low enough that you feel it in your chest before you hear it.

  2. 02

    The floor

    You drop further than you can get on your own. Near the end you can sit with one other person in silence for a few minutes, or stay where you are. Both are ordinary choices in this room.

  3. 03

    Return

    You come back up, the lights come up a little, and the night is over. Most people leave lighter than they came in, and some leave without something they had carried in for a long time.
