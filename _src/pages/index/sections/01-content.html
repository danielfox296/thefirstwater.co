The night, plainly

You lie down on a mat among twenty or so people who came for the same reason you did. For the better part of an hour there is nothing to do and nothing to say, just sound, most of it pitched low enough that you feel it in your chest before you hear it. Near the end there is an invitation to sit with one other person in silence for a few minutes, and if you would rather stay on the floor, that is what the floor is for. Then the sound brings you back up, and the night is over.
