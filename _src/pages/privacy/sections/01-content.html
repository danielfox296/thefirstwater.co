Privacy

What I collect, and what I do with it.

Join the email list and I keep your email address. When you write to me through the contact form, I keep your name, your email, and what you wrote, so I can answer.

The site uses Google Analytics, which counts visits with cookies and shows me which pages get read. When you buy a ticket, payment runs through Stripe. Your card number goes to Stripe, never to me; what I see is your name, your email, and the seat you bought.

I use your email to tell you when a session goes up. I don't sell your information, and I don't share it beyond the services above that make the site run.

Every email I send has an unsubscribe link at the bottom. Use it and you're off the list.

If you have a question about any of this, or want what I have about you deleted, write to me through the contact form.

Firstwater operates from Littleton, Colorado.
